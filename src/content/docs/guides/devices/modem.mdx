A **modem** (modulator-demodulator) converts digital data from your network to analog signals for transmission over telephone or cable lines, and vice versa.

> It operates at **Layer 1 (Physical)** and enables internet connectivity.

---

## How It Works

The modem **modulates** outgoing digital data into analog for transmission, and **demodulates** incoming analog signals back into digital form.
